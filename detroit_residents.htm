It is cheap and easy to collect and store large amounts of data. (It's already being done, just in a fragmented manner.) This chart shows it would be easy to: 1) create a record for every resident in Detroit (starting with, say, city income tax return) 2) determine those that are making a lot of money, had a nice 401k, stock, etc.; those that are middle class; and those that are struggling and hurting other people around them 3) design policies and provided city services that measurably made everyone richer and better.
